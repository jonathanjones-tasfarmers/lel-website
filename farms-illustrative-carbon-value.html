⚠ Not actual carbon credits, none have been issued or sold

Why? ▾

No carbon credits (ACCUs or otherwise) have been issued, held, or sold in connection with this trial. Generating real, tradeable credits requires formal project registration, an approved methodology, and independent third-party verification under a scheme such as Australia's ACCU Scheme, none of which this project has undertaken.

The figure below is a purely illustrative calculation: this trial's own modelled CO2‑e abatement estimate, multiplied by a current market reference price, to show roughly what scale of value comparable verified abatement might represent if it were ever formally registered. It is not income, revenue, or an asset this project holds.

More info ▾

Reference price: AUD $30 to $35 per tonne CO2‑e (midpoint used), the range EY's Australian Carbon Market Outlook 2026 projects ACCU prices to hold through at least 2028. Actual market prices fluctuate, secondary-market ACCU trades have been reported as high as A$42/tonne around compliance deadlines. CO2‑e figures use the AR5 100-year GWP factor (28), consistent with Tasmania's official greenhouse gas reporting. Whether this trial's methodology would qualify for a registered ACCU method has not been assessed.
