About this premium ▾

No established market premium currently exists for verified low-emissions or low-methane wool. This field is left blank deliberately rather than defaulted, so the calculator doesn't assert a premium that hasn't been proven in the market.

For context: Responsible Wool Standard (RWS) certified wool (a welfare and traceability certification, not an emissions certification) has recently traded at premiums of roughly 20c to 150c per kg clean, currently sitting around 70c/kg clean (Mecardo, April 2026). Clean-to-greasy yield (typically 60–70%) would reduce this further on a greasy basis. Treat this as the closest available comparable, not a low-emissions wool price.

About this calculator ▾

This is a fixed-value, desktop-study calculator, not built from live LEL trial data. No wool trial is currently running under this project. Treat every figure here as a planning estimate to stress-test, not a guarantee.

  • SeaFeed cost defaults are sourced from TasFarmers' own TLEL project budget (Supplement Costs tab), not a published market rate.
  • The assumption that SeaFeed doesn't reduce fleece weight or quality rests on one 120-day farm pilot (Sea Forest / Congi Farm / Theory's "Regal Wool" program, reported April 2026), not a controlled trial or peer-reviewed study. Treat it as a stated assumption, not a proven result.
  • No established price-premium market exists for verified low-emissions or low-methane wool. The RWS comparison shown is the closest real, tradeable analogue available, but RWS certifies animal welfare and traceability, not emissions.
  • The 43% methane reduction co-benefit figure comes from the same single pilot above, not from independently replicated research specific to this project's flocks.
